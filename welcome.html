FaveDay is a diary with beautiful stats.

Every day you rate yourself with a score and write down some notes. FaveDay tracks these and shows you beautiful plots that can give you new insights into your life.

Open Folder View Demo Account
 

Local

Faveday saves data on your harddrive. Your data remains with you. If you use cloud synch with that folder is up to you.

Easy

No separate account is necessary to use FaveDay. Just connect via Dropbox and you're good to go. No need to sign with a signup form.

Secure

All your entries remain stored in simple text files in your secure Dropbox folder. FaveDay only has access to that folder and can't access any other data in your Dropbox.
